About Sweet And Savory Mini Donuts

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Sweet And Savory Mini Donuts was born out of a love for all things miniature and flavorful. Our founders, Juanito Calderon, John Mel C. Calderon and Melanie Calderon, embarked on a mission to create an oasis for donut enthusiasts. Their culinary expertise and dedication to perfection led to the creation of a haven where sweet meets savory.

Our Mini Donuts

At Sweet And Savory, we take the humble mini donut to new heights. Our menu boasts an array of mouthwatering flavors, carefully designed to tickle your taste buds. From timeless classics like Chocolate to bold and innovative combinations like Ube Langka, each mini donut is a testament to our commitment to flavor and creativity.

Quality Assurance

We firmly believe that quality is the heart of every great mini donut. That's why we use only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients. Our dough is freshly prepared every day, ensuring that each batch is light, airy, and delightful. Our toppings are selected with care to guarantee the utmost freshness and flavor.

Visit Us

Step into our cozy little shop, conveniently located at University Heights. The warm and inviting atmosphere, coupled with our friendly staff, makes every visit a memorable one. Whether you're stopping by for a quick snack, a delightful dessert, or planning a special event, we're here to make your day sweeter and more savory.

Join the Sweet And Savory Experience

Sweet And Savory Mini Donuts is more than just a place to indulge; it's an experience that ignites your taste buds and warms your heart. Join us in celebrating the art of the mini donut, and let us become your go-to destination for delightful moments.
